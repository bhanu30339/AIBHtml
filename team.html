His leadership reflects a commitment to inclusive growth, community empowerment, and strengthening people-to-people ties between Victoria and India.

Commitment to Governance & Transparency

The Australia India Bridge Foundation operates with strong governance principles, transparency, and accountability. Our leadership and advisory bodies work collaboratively to ensure integrity, inclusivity, and long-term impact across all initiatives.

Strategic Context

The Foundation’s work is aligned with the priorities outlined in Victoria’s India Strategy 2025–2030, focusing on economic cooperation, education and innovation, cultural and community engagement, sustainability, gender equality, and inclusive growth. Our leadership continues to support long-term partnerships bridging governments, businesses, and people across Victoria and India.
