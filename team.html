Deepak Vinayak OAM JP

Founder & Chairperson

Deepak Vinayak OAM JP is the Founder and Chairperson of the AusInd Bridge Foundation. He has played a significant role in shaping Australia–India engagement, including contributions aligned with Victoria's long-term India Strategy. His leadership reflects a commitment to inclusive growth, community empowerment, and strengthening people-to-people ties between Victoria and India.

Prof. Srinivas Subbarao

Founding Member & Academic Leader

Prof. Srinivas Subbarao is a distinguished Indian academic and a founding member of the Foundation. He is widely recognised for pioneering work in fostering campus startup and innovation culture across India. His contributions include national recognition and a Government of India higher education leadership fellowship at the University of Cambridge. He is currently a visiting professor at Curtin University.
