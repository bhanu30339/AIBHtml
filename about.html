Bridging Nations,
Building Futures

The alliance is established to foster collaboration, mutual growth, and social impact between Australia and India. With a strategic focus on business and bilateral trade, political strengthening, and charitable causes, the alliance brings together businesses, investors, politicians, and philanthropists to drive positive change.

Our Mission

Strengthen Australia-India ties across business & trade, political cooperation, and charitable causes to facilitate economic collaborations and uplift communities.
