Recent Event

Hosting Hon. Dylan Wight MP and Hon. Mathew Hilakari MP from Victoria, Australia

I had the honour of hosting Hon. Dylan Wight MP and Hon. Mathew Hilakari MP from Victoria, Australia, as part of the Visiting Australian Delegation. Organised in collaboration with the AusInd Bridge Foundation, this gathering brought together policymakers, business leaders, academics, innovators & young changemakers from both our nations.

"My heartfelt thanks to Deepak Vinayak OAM JP, Founder, AusInd Bridge Foundation (Australia), Dr. Jasvinder Sidhu, Federation University (Australia) and Prof. Kamala Kanta Dash (कृष्णकृपा), Rajasthan Centre of Advanced Technology (R-CAT), for shaping this meaningful dialogue and building bridges that matter."

The journey of #TechBridgeDiplomacy has just begun - and the road ahead is full of promise, purpose, and partnership.
