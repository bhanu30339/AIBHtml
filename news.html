CPA Australia signs MoU with Australia India Alliance Foundation at conference celebrating benefits of global trade

CPA Australia and the Australia India Alliance Foundation signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) as a sign of future collaboration to strengthen business ties between the two nations. This landmark partnership was announced at a special conference focused on the benefits of global trade.
