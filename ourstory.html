Our Story

A Journey of Vision and Collaboration

From a bold idea to becoming the leading voice for Australia-India bilateral relations—discover how we've shaped the partnership between two great nations.

How It All Began

A Vision for Stronger Ties

In 2010, a group of Australian and Indian business leaders came together with a shared vision: to create a platform that would strengthen the economic, cultural, and diplomatic ties between their two nations.

They recognized that while both countries shared democratic values, similar legal systems, and complementary economies, there was no dedicated organization focused on building bridges between leaders and institutions.

The Australia India Bridge Council was born from this vision—a commitment to facilitate meaningful connections, promote bilateral trade, and advocate for policies that benefit both nations.

What started as a small network of passionate individuals has grown into the premier platform for Australia-India engagement, with over 500 member organizations and a track record of facilitating billions of dollars in trade.

Our Journey

Key Moments in Our History

From our founding to the present day, these milestones mark our growth and impact.

2010

Foundation

The Australia India Bridge Council was established in Melbourne by a group of visionary business leaders and diplomats who recognized the immense potential of Australia-India relations.

2012

First Leadership Summit

Hosted our inaugural Australia-India Leadership Summit in Sydney, bringing together over 200 business leaders and government officials from both nations.

2014

India Office Launch

Expanded our presence with the opening of our New Delhi representative office, strengthening our on-ground capabilities in India.

2016

Trade Mission Success

Led our largest trade delegation to India with 50 Australian companies, resulting in over $500 million in commercial agreements.

2018

Government Partnership

Signed memorandum of understanding with Austrade and Invest India, formalizing our role in facilitating bilateral trade and investment.

2020

Digital Transformation

Launched our virtual engagement platform in response to global challenges, enabling continued connection and collaboration.

2022

Record Growth

Achieved milestone of 500+ member organizations and facilitated trade worth over $2 billion since inception.

2024

Strategic Partnership Era

Playing a pivotal role in the enhanced Australia-India strategic partnership, focusing on technology, clean energy, and education sectors.

Looking Ahead

The Next Chapter

As we look to the future, we are more committed than ever to our mission. The Australia-India relationship is at a pivotal moment, with unprecedented opportunities in technology, clean energy, education, and healthcare.

We are expanding our programs, deepening our partnerships, and investing in new capabilities to serve our members and stakeholders better. Together, we will continue to build bridges that benefit both nations for generations to come.
