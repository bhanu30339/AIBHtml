Trade Composition Analysis

Australian Exports to India (65%)

Top export categories that dominate Australia's exports to India:

  • Minerals & Ores - Key resources supporting India's manufacturing sector
  • Agricultural Products - High-quality produce and food items
  • Educational Services - World-class education and training
  • Energy Resources - Coal, LNG, and renewable energy technologies

Indian Exports to Australia (70%)

Major categories representing India's exports to Australia:

  • Textiles & Apparel - Traditional and modern textile products
  • Pharmaceuticals - Affordable medicines and healthcare products
  • Automotive Parts - Components for Australia's automotive industry
  • IT Services - Software, IT consulting, and digital solutions
  • Gems & Jewelry - Precious stones and crafted jewelry

Strategic Approach to Business Development

The Australia India Alliance adopts a comprehensive set of strategies to effectively foster business collaboration and strengthen economic ties:

Multi-Stakeholder Engagement

Engaging diverse stakeholders including government agencies, businesses, educational institutions, and industry associations to align interests and co-create solutions.

Networking & Collaboration Events

Organizing networking events, conferences, and trade fairs to facilitate meaningful interactions between businesses, policymakers, and investors from both countries.

Strategic Partnerships

Forging partnerships with like-minded organizations, think tanks, and research institutions to exchange knowledge and jointly work on specific projects.
