Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how the AusInd Bridge Foundation collects, uses, and protects information you provide when using our website.

Information We Collect

  • Contact information you provide when contacting us or subscribing to newsletters.
  • Non-identifying site usage data for analytics purposes.

How We Use Information

We use information to respond to enquiries, provide newsletters, and improve site functionality. We do not share personal data with third parties except where required by law.

Cookies

We use cookies for basic functionality and analytics. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Contact

For privacy queries, email info@ausindbridge.org.

FIRST NATIONS ACKNOWLEDGEMENT

The AusInd Bridge Foundation acknowledges the Traditional Custodians of the lands on which we operate across Australia. We pay our respects to Elders past, present and emerging, and recognise their continuing connection to land, waters and culture. We honour the rich cultural heritage of First Nations peoples and their enduring contribution to Australian society.

Building bridges between Australian and Indian leaders through diplomacy, trade, and cultural exchange. Fostering stronger bilateral relations through strategic partnerships and collaborative initiatives.
