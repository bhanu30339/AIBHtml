FIRST NATIONS ACKNOWLEDGEMENT

The Australia India Bridge Foundation acknowledges the Traditional Custodians of the lands on which we operate across Australia. We pay our respects to Elders past, present and emerging, and recognise their continuing connection to land, waters and culture. We honour the rich cultural heritage of First Nations peoples and their enduring contribution to Australian society.

Building bridges between Australian and Indian leaders through diplomacy, trade, and cultural exchange. Fostering stronger bilateral relations through strategic partnerships and collaborative initiatives.
