Our Vision

The Australia India Alliance envisions a dynamic and harmonious partnership between Australia and India, transcending geographical boundaries. Our vision is to build a resilient and inclusive alliance that fosters sustainable growth, mutual prosperity, and social well-being for both nations.

Our Mission

Our mission is to strengthen the ties between Australia and India across three core pillars:

  • Business & Bilateral Trade: Enhancing economic cooperation and commercial partnerships
  • Political Cooperation: Fostering diplomatic relations and policy alignment
  • Charitable Causes: Supporting social welfare and community development initiatives

Our Purpose

This is an invitation to join the Australia India Alliance as valuable stakeholders, partners, and supporters. We aim to share our vision, showcase our achievements, and present opportunities for collaboration and growth that the alliance offers.

"Together, we can forge a path towards a thriving future, built on mutual respect, unity, and shared success."

Parliamentarians Forum for Tech Diplomacy Launch

Honouring Australian Parliamentarians & Launching Global Tech Diplomacy Initiative

Recent Event

Hosting Hon. Dylan Wight MP and Hon. Mathew Hilakari MP from Victoria, Australia

I had the honour of hosting Hon. Dylan Wight MP and Hon. Mathew Hilakari MP from Victoria, Australia, as part of the Visiting Australian Delegation. Organised in collaboration with the AusInd Bridge Foundation, this gathering brought together policymakers, business leaders, academics, innovators & young changemakers from both our nations.

One of the most significant outcomes of the evening was the launch of the Parliamentarians Forum for Tech Diplomacy - a global initiative that aims to foster collaboration among legislators on the responsible and inclusive use of emerging technologies.

I emphasized how diplomacy today must go beyond convention and become participatory, and tech enabled. The next wave in diplomacy is in its democratization. As a fantastic case study, I highlighted how Hon'ble Prime Minister Narendra Modi ji's G20 model of Diplomacy ensured multi-stakeholder engagements across 60 cities and 28 states, stands as a powerful inspiration for inclusive global leadership.
