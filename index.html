Core Values

The guiding principles that shape our mission and define our impact across Australia and India.

01

Integrity

Building trust through honest, transparent, and ethical actions in every interaction.

02

Inclusivity

Celebrating diversity and ensuring every voice is valued across both nations.

03

Collaboration

Cultivating partnerships across governments, businesses, and communities for meaningful impact.

04

Innovation

Embracing new ideas and approaches to drive continuous improvement and growth.

05

Empowerment

Uplifting individuals and communities to reach their fullest potential.

06

Sustainability

Committing to long-lasting environmental and social impact for future generations.

07

Resilience

Facing challenges with courage and maintaining perseverance in pursuing our mission.

08

Excellence

Striving for the highest standards to ensure transformative outcomes in all endeavors.

Founder

Deepak Vinayak OAM JP

Founder & Chairperson

Deepak Vinayak OAM JP is the Founder and Chairperson of the Australia India Bridge Foundation. He has played a significant role in shaping Australia–India engagement, including contributions aligned with Victoria’s long-term India Strategy. His leadership reflects a commitment to inclusive growth, community empowerment, and strengthening people-to-people ties between Victoria and India.
