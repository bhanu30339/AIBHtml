Strategic initiatives designed to create sustainable impact across education, healthcare, environment, and community development

Social Causes Beyond Charity

Social impact at the Australia India Alliance extends beyond charitable giving. Our work focuses on strengthening people-to-people connections, preserving culture, building skills, and enabling long-term community resilience across Australia and India.

Education, Skills & Innovation

Supporting education pathways, language learning, research collaboration, and skills development that empower future generations and strengthen bilateral innovation.

Culture, Community & People

Celebrating Indian culture in Australia through festivals, arts, community programs, and diaspora engagement that foster social cohesion.

Tourism & Connectivity

Strengthening tourism, city-to-city partnerships, and regional engagement to promote economic and cultural exchange between Australia and India.

Social Equity & Inclusion

Advancing gender equality, multicultural inclusion, and equal opportunity through policy-aligned community initiatives.

Strategic & Policy Alignment

Social and community initiatives are closely aligned with broader political and strategic engagement between Australia and India, ensuring sustainable, long-term impact beyond individual programs.

Political & Strategic Engagement

Supporting diplomatic dialogue, parliamentary exchanges, and policy cooperation that reinforce mutual trust and shared values.

Multilateral & Regional Cooperation

Engagement through regional forums, city-level partnerships, and international collaboration across education, trade, and sustainability.

Diaspora-Led Partnerships

Leveraging the strength of the Indian diaspora to drive social, economic, and cultural connections across communities and regions.

VICTORIAN GOVERNMENT INITIATIVES

Strengthening Community Connections

Connecting Schools with Indian Communities

Supports Victorian primary and secondary schools to engage the Indian diaspora and develop students' understanding of the contributions the diaspora makes to our State.

Impact: Nearly 3000 students in Victorian schools are studying Indian languages with $3.5 million committed in the 2023-24 State Budget.

Cultural Funding & Support

Since 2014, the Victorian Government's Indian cultural affairs portfolio has provided over $38 million in funding for projects supporting Victoria's Indian Community.

Events: Supporting festivals like the Indian Film Festival of Melbourne and Festival of Diwali at Federation Square.

Regional Community Development

Victoria's regions, particularly Bendigo and Geelong, have growing Indian communities with Indian events becoming a fixture in local calendars.

Example: Bendigo's Holi Festival and Geelong's Pako Festa showcase cultural integration.

Flagship Community Development Programs

Bendigo Community Integration

The City of Greater Bendigo enjoys a growing relationship with India through the city's Indian community, cultural, education and tourism connections.

Key Programs

La Trobe University campus offering courses in dental science, nursing, IT and biomedical science for Indian students

Cultural Events

Holi Festival and regional launch of Indian Film Festival of Melbourne

Economic Contribution

Indian-born community contributes significantly to health, engineering, IT, and food industry sectors

Geelong Global Engagement

Geelong has established itself as a vibrant hub for economic and cultural development, with strong education and innovation connections to India.

Education Hub

Deakin University has campuses in both Geelong and India, making the city a premier destination for Indian students

Cultural Integration

Pako Festa, Victoria's largest free multicultural festival, attracts more than 50,000 visitors annually

Innovation Connection

Advanced Fibre Cluster connected with India through international research partnerships
