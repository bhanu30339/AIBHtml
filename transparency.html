Transparency & Disclosure

We are committed to transparency in our governance, funding, and partnerships. This page provides high-level information about our funding sources, governance structure, and conflict-of-interest policies.

Funding

We receive support from a mix of philanthropic donations, membership contributions, and project-based grants. Specific funding information is disclosed where appropriate in project materials.

Governance

Our governance is overseen by the Board and Advisory Council. Board members' professional affiliations are listed on the Team page.

Conflicts of Interest

We request disclosure of potential conflicts from board members and partners and manage them according to our internal policy.
