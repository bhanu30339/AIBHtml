Transparency & Disclosure

We are committed to openness in our governance, funding, and partnerships. This page provides detailed information about how we operate, our financial transparency, and our commitment to ethical standards.

Funding & Financial Transparency

We maintain transparency in all financial matters and receive support from diverse sources to ensure independence and sustainability.

  • Philanthropic Donations: From individuals and foundations supporting our mission
  • Membership Contributions: From our corporate and individual members
  • Project-based Grants: For specific initiatives and programs
  • Event Sponsorships: For conferences, seminars, and cultural events

Specific funding information is disclosed in all project materials and annual reports to maintain accountability.

Governance Structure

Our organization is governed by a diverse board and advisory council with expertise in international relations, business, and cultural exchange.

  • Board of Directors: Responsible for strategic direction and oversight
  • Advisory Council: Provides expert guidance on key initiatives
  • Executive Team: Manages day-to-day operations and program implementation
  • Audit Committee: Ensures financial integrity and compliance

Board members' professional affiliations and biographies are publicly available on our Team page.

Conflict of Interest Policy

We maintain strict policies to identify, disclose, and manage any potential conflicts of interest.

  • Annual disclosure requirements for all board members and senior staff
  • Independent review of potential conflicts by the Governance Committee
  • Recusal procedures for decision-making where conflicts exist
  • Transparent reporting of partnerships and affiliations

Our comprehensive conflict of interest policy is available upon request and reviewed annually.

Governance Structure Details

Board of Directors

Responsible for strategic oversight, financial stewardship, and ensuring the organization fulfills its mission. The board meets quarterly and includes experts from Australia and India.

Advisory Council

Provides specialized guidance on program development, partnerships, and policy matters. Members serve two-year terms and represent diverse sectors and geographic regions.

Executive Leadership

Manages day-to-day operations, program implementation, and stakeholder relations. The executive team reports to the Board of Directors and implements strategic decisions.
